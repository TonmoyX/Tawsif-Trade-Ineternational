Our Hajj Services

Hajj Journey Steps

  • Pre-Departure Preparation

    Complete documentation, attend orientation sessions, and receive your Hajj kit with essential items.

  • Arrival in Saudi Arabia

    Welcome reception at the airport, transfer to your hotel, and initial briefing about the upcoming rituals.

  • Stay in Makkah

    Comfortable accommodation near Haram, performance of Umrah, and preparation for Hajj rituals.

  • Hajj Rituals

    Guided performance of all Hajj rituals including stays in Mina, Arafat, and Muzdalifah with full support.

  • Visit to Madinah

    Travel to Madinah, visit to Masjid Al-Nabawi, and historical sites with expert guidance.

Hajj Requirements

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months validity
  • Recent passport-size photographs with white background
  • Completed Hajj application form
  • Valid Meningitis vaccination certificate
  • COVID-19 vaccination certificate (as per current requirements)
  • Marriage certificate (for couples)
  • Bank statement showing adequate funds
  • Police clearance certificate
  • Properly filled out health declaration form
